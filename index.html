About Me

Hi, my name is Varun. I'm a student passionate about embedded systems and hardware design, exploring the intersection of software and hardware. I work with microcontrollers, design PCBs, and write firmware that brings circuits to life. Based in Mumbai, I spend my time coding in C/C++, designing circuits, and figuring out how to make hardware do interesting things.

My interests span bootloader development, wireless communication protocols, custom development boards, and robotics. The most satisfying moment is when a board I've designed powers on successfully for the first time.

When I'm not working on projects, I enjoy learning about new embedded technologies, and contributing to open-source hardware projects.
